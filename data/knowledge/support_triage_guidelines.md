\# Support Triage Guidelines



Support issues should be analyzed by ticket category, sentiment, priority, and resolution status.



Negative sentiment combined with repeated unresolved tickets is a strong churn risk signal.



Recommended support triage process:

\- Prioritize high-priority tickets with negative sentiment.

\- Escalate unresolved billing and technical issues.

\- Review accounts with multiple support tickets in the last 90 days.

\- Use support trends to identify operational bottlenecks.

\- Coordinate with customer success when support issues overlap with churned customers.
