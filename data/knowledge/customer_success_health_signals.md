\# Customer Success Health Signals



Customer health should be monitored using product usage, active users, support sentiment, and renewal risk.



Low product usage is a warning sign when customers reduce engagement before renewal dates.



Important signals:

\- Declining feature usage

\- Low health score

\- Negative support sentiment

\- Billing complaints

\- Upcoming renewal with unresolved issues

\- Churn concentration in a specific region



Recommended actions:

\- Create a watchlist for low-health customers.

\- Trigger outreach when usage drops.

\- Review renewal risk for enterprise accounts.

\- Combine product usage trends with support data before recommending retention actions.
