\# Retention Playbook



High-risk customers should be prioritized when they show churn signals such as reduced product usage, repeated support tickets, billing complaints, or negative sentiment.



For regional churn spikes, customer success teams should first identify the affected region, top cancellation reason, and revenue impact.



Recommended actions:

\- Launch targeted retention campaigns for the highest-churn region.

\- Assign customer success managers to high-value accounts.

\- Offer billing review calls when billing issues are the leading cancellation reason.

\- Track health score changes weekly.

\- Escalate enterprise accounts with negative support sentiment.
