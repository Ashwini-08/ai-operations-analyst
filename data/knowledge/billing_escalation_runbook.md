\# Billing Escalation Runbook



Billing issues are a high-priority churn driver when they appear as a top cancellation reason or repeated support complaint.



Billing-related churn should be handled with urgency when annualized revenue loss exceeds $1M.



Recommended workflow:

\- Review recent billing support tickets.

\- Identify whether complaints involve pricing, invoice errors, failed payments, or contract confusion.

\- Route unresolved billing escalations to finance operations within 24 hours.

\- Provide customer-facing explanation and resolution timeline.

\- For enterprise customers, assign an account manager to coordinate the billing review.
